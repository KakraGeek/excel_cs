Admission Requirements

Admission to all academic levels is highly competitive. Admission forms can be purchased and filled online or from the administration office. Completed forms can be emailed or hand-delivered to the office of the school administrator.

The following documents must accompany the admission form.

  • A copy of student’s birth certificate
  • A copy of child’s immunization card (weighing card)
  • A passport sized photo

The school conducts entrance examinations in Mathematics and English language for students seeking admission to Grade 1 through to Junior High School. For admission into Kindergarten, it is a requirement for the child to be assessed in English and Mathematics.

  • Children seeking admission to the Primary or J.H.S are required to provide their cumulative Record Booklet from their former school.
